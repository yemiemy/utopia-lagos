Our Story

Utopia is the world's first urban innovation group focused solely on emerging cities and their slums.

We got our feet wet in cities in Asia years ago and over time, realized that innovation and entrepreneurship could bring a new perspective to quickly growing megacities around the world. We moved from Asia into San Francisco to set up what would eventually come to be known as Utopia and are focused on building an urban innovation ecosystem across Africa, Asia, and Latin America.
